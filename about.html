The Ministry

The Ministry Foundations

We believe that these are ‘Essentials for Revival’:
THE 5 MIGHTIES. It is written, “Thy seed shall be mighty in the land.” Psalm 112:2a

Mighty in the word
To know, believe, and obey the Bible: this informs the other four foundations.
What does being mighty in the word look like?
Matt.8:8, 16; Luke 4:32, 36; Ps.33:4, 6, 9; Heb.4:12

Mighty in prayer
To fellowship with God, developing love for prayer
What does being mighty in prayer look like?
Answered! John 11:41-42; Psalm 22:21b (NKJV); Psalm 38:15; Psalm 65:2; Psalm 84:8

Mighty in praise
To thank God, aloud: He is good, merciful, faithful, righteous, just.
What does being mighty in praise look like?
Inhabited by God! Psalm 22:3

Mighty in giving
To give our time, our talent/gift, our treasure.
What does being mighty in giving look like? Cheerful 2Cor.9:7; willing Ex.25:2; 1Chron.29:14, 16-18; out of abundance 2Cor.8:14

Mighty in singing
To sing to God, aloud, with all musical instruments, and including new songs.
What does being mighty in singing look like? Psalm 33:3; Psalm 92:1-3; Psalm 144:9; Psalm 149:1; Isa.42:10; Job 35:10; Psalm 42:8
