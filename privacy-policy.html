Privacy Policy

At GOWE CENTER, we respect, and will do our best to protect the privacy of anyone who provides us with any personally identifiable information. We know that protecting your personal information is very important to you (as it is to us!) so we have developed this Privacy Policy to help explain how we handle any personal information you may share with us.
GOWE CENTER will only collect, use, and share your personal information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

WHAT WE COLLECT, AND HOW WE USE IT

We do not collect information about you when you visit our website unless you choose to provide that information to us. All personal information collected by GOWE CENTER is done so exclusively with your consent, by means of a form posted on our website. No information is collected automatically.

If you subscribe to our Blog, we ask for information to identify you as a member. You are required to provide your email address. We maintain an opt-out, subscription database of users who have requested to receive these emails. Every email provides an option for you to unsubscribe to such information. We do not sell or share your information with anyone else.

We do not collect your email for other purposes. We do not sell or share your information with anyone else.

UNSUBSCRIBE

Once subscribed, we will use your email address to send the content to you. If you no longer wish to receive our emails, you may follow the unsubscribe instructions contained in each of the communications you receive.

OTHER MATERIAL YOU SUBMIT ONLINE

With the exception of the information we have agreed to protect in our Privacy Policy, any other suggestion, material or information that you send to GOWE CENTER through our website will be considered non-confidential and non-proprietary.

If we reproduce and/or adapt these suggestions, material or information for any purpose we deem appropriate to GOWE CENTER we are under no obligation to pay you and/or any other person(s) for their use. We will also have exclusive ownership of all current and future rights for anything that GOWE CENTER produces which includes the suggestions, material or information you send to us.

TESTIMONIES AND IMAGES

We post testimonies and images on our website, which may contain personal information. If you wish to request the removal of your testimony, please contact us.

LINKS TO OTHER WEBSITES

Our website contains links to other websites. Should you choose to follow a link to another website, you are subject to its privacy policy. GOWE CENTER is not responsible for the privacy policies or the content of these other websites. We encourages you to carefully read the privacy policy of any website you visit. This privacy policy applies only to information collected by our website.

UPDATES TO PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We encourage you to check this Privacy Policy from time to time to remain informed of any changes as your continued use of our website indicates your acceptance of these changes.

EFFECTIVE DATE

This version of the Privacy Policy became effective on 24 August 2019.
