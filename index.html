GOD’S REST

Psalm 132:14

Posted by Pastor Joel Mrima on Aug. 22, 2019, 8:23 p.m.

TESTIMONIES

Elizabeth Mbithe
Church Member

In this church,I found a home. I found love. I found hope. I have found a family. I have been blessed through the ministry of our Bishop and pastors. I thank the Lord for this church. They are my family.

SISTER RUTH KIHURANI
Church Member

Bishop Evans Mrima led me through the sinner’s prayer and I was born again. God in his faithfulness has kept me in salvation to-date. I have learned many things about the word of God and the simple knowledge that God watches over His word to perform it has coined my life. I have witnessed great moments of triumph in the power that is in the name, ‘Jesus’. Indeed, as the bible says, ‘The Name of the Lord is a strong and Mighty Tower and when the righteous run into this name they are safe’.

SISTER EMILY BORE
Church Member

I joined this ministry as a result of some friends who visited me. The joy during praise and worship put me at ease. These were miracles taking place and many people where testifying of what the Lord has done for them. I received a miracle of healing from ulcer that I had suffered for a long time. I have been taught to stand by the word of God, to know the word and do it. It has made a great impact in my life, my family and my progress in life. I can confidently say this far God has been our Ebenezer.
